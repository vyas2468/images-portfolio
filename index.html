About Me

I have had an interest in web technologies for several years but it was only recently that I started to take it seriously!

I come from a Finance background and the last company I was contracting with decided to move operations earlier in 2018 elsewhere, which led to my resignation. Since learning the fundamentals of programming, I have built working knowledge of several technologies and have begun working on my portfolio to showcase to you. I really enjoy working on web technologies and taking an idea to end product.
